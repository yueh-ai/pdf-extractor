## Deduplication Notes

- I merged repeated completion references for TD, PBTD, casing shoes, tubing/packer depth, and perforation interval when the same value appeared in the operations narrative and later completion forms.
- I treated proposed, revised, and actual plan values as separate records when they changed materially, especially for TD, KOP, casing shoes, DV tool depth, and TOC.
- I used the page 20 rendered image to resolve a markdown/OCR artifact in the directional plot table: the visible KOP label is 5,647.54', matching page 19 and the page 18 revised narrative, not 5,847.54'.
- I deduplicated the perforation interval from summary forms on pages 25 and 28 against the detailed operation/perforation lists on pages 26, 27, and 30. The deduplicated top and bottom perforations are 6,384' and 10,434'.
- I kept the page 23 kick-off plug interval, tagged cement depth, and slide-to-start depth as separate reference items because they serve different field meanings even though they describe the same kick-off operation.
- I found no float shoe, bridge plug, retainer, CIBP, or cast-iron bridge plug references in the allowed pages. The allowed pages do contain a float collar, isolation plug, kick-off plug, flow-through plugs, and an AS-1 packer/tubing reference.
