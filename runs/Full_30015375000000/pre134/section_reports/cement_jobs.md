## Deduplication Notes

Page 1 is the original APD proposed casing/cement program; it was not merged into actual rows because the actual casing depths and cement volumes changed. Page 3 repeats high-level plan language about setting and cementing either 4-1/2 in or 5-1/2 in production casing but gives no cement volumes, so it was treated as context rather than a separate cement row.

Pages 7 and 18 are separate contingency plan versions. They were kept as distinct proposed rows because both the setting depths and cement volumes changed. The page 7 image was used as authority where the markdown missed the 675 sx PVL line and misread the DV tool text.

Pages 22 and 23 provide the detailed actual casing, plug, and production casing cement reports. Pages 25 and 28 repeat the actual casing/cement summary in C-104/C-105 tables; those summary rows were merged into the actual rows as confirmation, except the 20 in conductor redi-mix row, which appears only in the completion-record summary.

Pages 26 and 27 provide completion-operation narrative, while page 30 provides the same acid/fracture treatment data in a cleaner C-105 attachment table. The completion-treatment rows use page 30 as the clean tabular duplicate and pages 26-27 as narrative support. No cement squeeze job was found in the bounded pages 1-133.
