## Deduplication Notes

The revised KOP values `5,647 ft` and `5,647.54 ft MD` were merged as the same planned kickoff depth because page 18 gives the rounded narrative value and page 19 gives the directional-plan table value. They were not merged with the original `6,483 ft` KOP because page 17 says a new directional plan was attached, and the revised pages changed both target TVD and TD.

The actual TD value `10,495 ft` was merged across the operations narrative, C-104/C-105 completion forms, and final survey because all cite the same final measured depth. The actual PBTD `10,440 ft` was merged across the C-104/C-105 forms and the completion-operations narrative because the values match exactly.

The actual lateral start was not collapsed into a single exact depth. The operation narrative says cement was tagged at `5,569 ft`, the crew slid to `5,710 ft`, and directional drilling started; the survey shows the well becomes near-horizontal by about `6,451 ft MD`. Those are different operational concepts, so the report preserves both.
