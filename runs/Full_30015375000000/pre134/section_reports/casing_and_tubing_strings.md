## Deduplication Notes

The actual final strings were merged from operation reports and completion forms. Page 21 and page 28 describe the same 20" conductor at 40'. Page 22 and page 28 describe the same actual 13-3/8" surface casing and 9-5/8" intermediate casing. Page 23 and page 28 describe the same actual 5-1/2" production casing. Page 27 and page 28 describe the same actual 2-7/8" tubing at 5,600', with page 27 supplying the tubing weight and grade.

The page 3 permit comments were treated as narrative duplicates of the original proposal and contingency design rather than separate strings, because page 1 and page 7 contain the tabular casing details. The original contingency design on page 7 and the revised change-plan design on page 18 were not merged because their material facts changed: target/set depths changed from 7,233'/11,375'/6,400' cut-pull to 6,400'/10,200'/5,600' cut-pull, and the revised plan added a 5-1/2" P-110/L-80 production design to 10,200'. Actual strings were kept separate from proposed/revised strings.
