## Deduplication Notes

Merged duplicate actual casing-record evidence from pages 25 and 28 for the 26", 17-1/2", 12-1/4", and 8-3/4" final completion rows because they repeat the same casing size, depth set, hole size, and cementing record. Page 22 was kept as the stronger actual operations source for the 17-1/2" and 12-1/4" TDs because it states the drilling sequence and hole-size reductions.

Merged page 22's "reduced hole to 8-3/4" and page 23's pilot/lateral operations into two separate 8-3/4" actual rows because they describe different wellbore paths: the 8,500' pilot hole and the final directional production hole from the kickoff/start-directional-drilling area to 10,495'. The completion records confirm the final 8-3/4" production hole but do not preserve the pilot-hole split, so the operations report and survey pages were used to keep that distinction.

Kept original proposed, revised proposed, and contingency rows when the diameter, TD, or change point changed. The original C-101/page 3 plan, page 18 revised plan, and page 23/28 actual records do not describe the same interval cleanly enough to collapse into one row without losing conflicts.
