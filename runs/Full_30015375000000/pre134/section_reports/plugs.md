## Deduplication Notes

I kept page 3, page 18, and page 23 kickoff-related entries separate because they represent original plan, revised plan, and actual execution rather than repeated reports of the same fact. The actual isolation plug and actual kickoff plug were extracted from the same 6/30/10 operation but kept as separate rows because they have different intervals and cement volumes.

I did not convert generic "plug and abandon" form checkboxes, C-115 "non-plugged well" rule text, or financial-assurance plugging language into plug rows because those are boilerplate or regulatory text without well-specific plug intervals. I also did not treat PBTD 10,440' as a physical plug row because the source labels it only as plug-back measured depth, not a plug setting.

Page 26's 5374'-5496' cement drillout was not merged into the kickoff plug because it occurs during later completion operations and references the DV tool. It is included as uncertain plug-like cement evidence because it may matter to wellbore drawing, but the source does not call it a plug.
